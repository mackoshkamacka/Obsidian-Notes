This replaced the [[War Measures Act - Canada]] to be harmonized with the Charter of Rights and Freedoms: 
Defines emergency situations (as a response to the overextension of the government's involvement in October Crisis): natural catastrophes: disease in humans, animals, or plants, accidents or pollution.  The limitations of government are: all measures are subject to approval by Parliament; and must take rights into consideration (compensation must be offered to anyone injured or damaged due to the act).
